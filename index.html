Who we are ?

Mother and Child
health care Association

Mother and Child health care Association is a Cameroon based charity organization, dedicated to standing up for families especially within the reproductive age, and children under the age of five years. With live experience, we advocate for the rights of every pregnant woman and children for proper health care and the right to live. Learn More
